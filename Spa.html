After a long period of lockdowns and restrictions due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many people are eager to return to the spa for some much-needed relaxation and self-care. However, there are a few things you should know as a client going back to the spa to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Here's what to keep in mind:

Check the Spa's Safety Protocols

Before booking your appointment, check the spa's website or call them to ask about their safety protocols. Many spas have implemented new measures, such as requiring clients and staff to wear masks, limiting the number of people in the spa at one time, and increasing cleaning and sanitization procedures.

Bring Your Own Supplies

To minimize contact and reduce the risk of transmission, consider bringing your own supplies, such as towels, robes, and slippers. If the spa provides these items, ask about their cleaning and sanitization procedures.

Be Prepared for Screening Questions

When you arrive at the spa, you may be asked screening questions about your health, recent travel, and exposure to COVID-19. Be prepared to answer these questions truthfully and provide any necessary information to ensure the safety of everyone in the spa.

Arrive on Time, but Not Too Early

To minimize the number of people in the spa at one time, many spas are asking clients to arrive at their scheduled appointment time and not earlier. However, it's still important to arrive on time to ensure that you have enough time for your treatment.

Wear a Mask

In most cases, clients are required to wear a mask while in the spa, except when receiving a facial or other treatment that requires the removal of the mask. Bring your own mask or ask the spa if they provide masks for clients.

Be Patient and Understanding

Spas are working hard to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for their clients, but there may be some changes and delays due to the new safety protocols. Be patient and understanding, and follow the spa's guidelines to ensure the safety of everyone in the spa.

To Note

My Persanal Opinion

Returning to the spa can be a great way to relax and rejuvenate, but it's important to be aware of the new safety protocols and guidelines. Check the spa's safety protocols, bring your own supplies, be prepared for screening questions, arrive on time but not too early, wear a mask, and be patient and understanding. By following these guidelines, you can enjoy a safe and enjoyable spa experience.

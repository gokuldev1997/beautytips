As the new year approaches, it's a great time to reflect on our skincare habits and make resolutions for healthier, glowing skin in the coming year. Here are ten skincare resolutions that you should make in 2022 to help you achieve your best skin yet.

Wear Sunscreen Every Day

One of the most crucial skincare resolutions you can make is to wear sunscreen every day. UV rays can cause premature aging, dark spots, and even skin cancer. Choose a broad-spectrum sunscreen with an SPF of at least 30 and apply it every morning as the last step in your skincare routine.

Cleanse Your Skin Thoroughly

Make sure to cleanse your skin thoroughly every day, especially at night. This removes dirt, oil, and impurities that can clog your pores and lead to breakouts. Use a gentle cleanser that's appropriate for your skin type and avoid using hot water, which can strip your skin of its natural oils.

Exfoliate Regularly

Exfoliating helps remove dead skin cells, revealing smoother, brighter skin. However, be careful not to over-exfoliate, which can damage your skin's barrier function. Choose a gentle exfoliant that's appropriate for your skin type and use it no more than once or twice a week.

Get Enough Sleep

Sleep is crucial for healthy skin, as it's when your body repairs and regenerates skin cells. Aim to get seven to eight hours of sleep each night, and try to establish a consistent sleep routine.

Be Mindful of Product Interactions

Be mindful of product interactions when layering skincare products. For example, if you use a product that contains AHAs or BHAs, wait at least 10 minutes before applying other products to avoid irritation. Similarly, avoid using vitamin C and retinol products together, as they can cancel out each other's benefits.

Take Care of Your Eye Area

The skin around your eyes is delicate and prone to wrinkles and fine lines. Incorporate an eye cream into your skincare routine to hydrate and protect this area.

Don't Forget Your Neck and Chest

The neck and chest are often neglected in skincare routines, but they are just as prone to aging as the face. Apply your skincare products, including sunscreen, to your neck and chest for complete protection.

Consult a Professional

If you have specific skincare concerns or are unsure of which products to use, consult a professional. A dermatologist or esthetician can provide personalized recommendations and treatments for your skin type and concerns.

My Persanal Opinion

Making these ten skincare resolutions a part of your routine can help you achieve healthier, glowing skin in the coming year. Remember to wear sunscreen, cleanse your skin thoroughly, and hydrate from the inside out. Incorporate antioxidants into your routine, be mindful of product interactions, and don't forget to take care of your eye area, neck, and chest. Lastly, don't hesitate to consult a professional for personalized recommendations and treatments. Here's to healthier, glowing skin.
